How Hypotenuse Leg Works and why S-S-A (Side-Side-Angle) doesn't

Hypotenuse Leg only works when the triangle is a right triangle and that is because the side that is opposite of the right angle will be the largest side, and this is the only instence when S-S-A(Side-Side-Angle) works.
